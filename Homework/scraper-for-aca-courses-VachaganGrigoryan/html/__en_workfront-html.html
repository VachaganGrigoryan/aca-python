About the Internship

Workfront is thrilled to announce the next Internship program in cooperation with Armenian Code Academy. This time the program will be devoted to Full-Stack Software Development. Graduates of Workfront internship programs are currently working at Workfront and other top Software Development companies as full-time Software Engineers. Please note that all Internship expenses will be covered by Workfront Armenia! We are creating the opportunity for you to invest time to your career development.
The internship will last 7 months and consists of 2 stages. During the first stage (5 months), you’ll learn different Front-end (JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, CSS) and Back-end (Java, Spring, SQL) technologies and will be working on exciting projects in small teams. The second stage (2 months) will be full-time, paid internship at Workfront! You will have the opportunity to work on Workfront products with our Product teams. The graduate Software Engineer will be ready to do Full-stack and Test development work.

What will you get from Workfront internship program?

-Knowledge of theoretical basics of Front-End, Back-End, and Test development work
-Opportunity to apply your knowledge by creating projects in small teams
-Interaction with highly skilled Software Engineers who’ll share their knowledge on daily basis
-Practical experience actually working on and contributing to Enterprise scale tech Product
-Consideration for any relevant opening at Workfront!

How long will the internship last?

-The duration is seven months.

Requirements to participate

-Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science is highly preferable
-Basic knowledge of any programming language is desired
-Knowledge of JavaScript is appreciated
-Some experience with Back-end technologies nice to have
-Initiative taking and self-motivated personality
-Ability to learn fast and eagerness to learn more
-Availability to work at least 25 hours in a week
-Strong desire of becoming a Software Engineer

What should you do to get selected for Workfront internship program?

-Dedicate time to apply for this opportunity
-Take preliminary Tech Assessment at ACA
-Be our guest at Workfront and tell us a bit about yourself
-Keep calm and feel free! :)
