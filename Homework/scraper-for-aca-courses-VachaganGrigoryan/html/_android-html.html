About the course

This course is designed for those who wish to learn Android application development. Our program combines theory and practice to help you build great apps from scratch. Course program has been based on requirements of leading IT companies, which will guarantee your future employment.

Please Note: Knowledge of Java programming concepts such as working with variables, arrays, functions and cycles is necessary in order to participate in this course. Your proficiency will be tested on the exam.

Education


Lectures and seminars are important components of our teaching method. After each lesson, students receive homework and lecture materials, which are available online in their personal account. In addition, during the training students will be accompanied not only by lecturers, but also by personal tutors, who will gladly answer all their questions after lessons.
